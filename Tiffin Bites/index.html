About Us

Welcome to Tiffin Bites, your go-to destination for delicious and authentic South Indian tiffins! We take pride in serving a wide variety of traditional dishes that are not only flavorful but also made with the finest ingredients. Our mission is to bring the taste of home-cooked South Indian meals to your table, ensuring that every bite is a delightful experience.

At Tiffin Bites, we understand the importance of quality and authenticity. Our chefs are passionate about South Indian cuisine and are dedicated to creating dishes that reflect the rich culinary heritage of the region. From crispy dosas to fluffy idlis, each dish is prepared with love and care, using traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Timings: 7:00 AM - 10:00 PM(All Days)

Location: Chaithanyapuri, Hyderabad, Telangana, India
